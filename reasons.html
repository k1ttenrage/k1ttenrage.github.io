Reasons why cats are silly

They love to play:

Cats are natural hunters, and their playful instincts often take over. This can lead to them chasing after anything that moves, including toys, bugs, or even your feet. Their playful pouncing and batting can be quite comical.

They're curious creatures:

Cats are always sniffing around and exploring their environment. This can sometimes lead them into getting stuck in tight spaces or getting startled by unexpected objects.

They don't understand physics (sometimes):

Cats have a knack for making gravity-defying leaps and landings. Sometimes these attempts are successful, but other times they result in some pretty funny tumbles.
